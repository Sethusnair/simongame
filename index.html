How To Play?
  1. Watch and Listen: Observe the sequence of lights and sounds Simon plays.
  2. Repeat the Sequence: Mimic the sequence by pressing the buttons in the same order.
  3. Advance Levels: Correctly repeat each sequence to progress to higher levels with longer sequences.
  4. Avoid Mistakes: Be precise; a wrong button press or order ends the game in some versions.
  5. High Scores: Aim to reach higher levels by accurately repeating longer sequences.
  6. Game Over: The game ends when you make a mistake (depending on the version) or choose to stop playing.
